Our Story

Green Fields Dairy Farm was founded in 2008 with a vision to deliver the purest and freshest dairy products to local families. We started with just 3 cows and a small shed, and today we’ve grown into a trusted brand across the region — all while keeping our roots in natural, ethical dairy farming.

Our Values

  • ✅ 100% natural and chemical-free feed
  • ✅ No artificial hormones or preservatives
  • ✅ Clean, ethical treatment of animals
  • ✅ Daily fresh delivery to your doorstep

Meet Our Farmer

Ravi Kumar is the heart behind Green Fields. With over 15 years of experience in organic dairy farming, he’s committed to producing milk you can trust — from cows that are cared for like family.
